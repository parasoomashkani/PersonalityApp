Wood Products

In 2020, China exported $15.3B in Wood Products, making it the 1st largest exporter of Wood Products in the world. At the same year, Wood Products was the 17th most exported product in China. The main destination of Wood Products exports from China are: United States ($3.06B), Japan ($1.37B), United Kingdom ($858M), Vietnam ($726M), and Germany ($654M).
